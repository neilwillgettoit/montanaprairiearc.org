Montana Prairie Amateur Radio Club

Welcome to our non-profit ham radio club! We meet every Thursday evening on the Baker, MT 147.060MHz Repeater.

If you're interested in amateur radio, whether you're a newcomer or a seasoned operator, we invite you to join us. Our club provides a friendly environment for sharing knowledge, learning, and enjoying the world of ham radio.

Key Details:

  • We meet every Thursday at 7:00 PM on the Baker, MT 147.060MHz Repeater.
  • We are a 501(c)3 non-profit organization dedicated to promoting amateur radio.
  • Join our mailing list to stay informed about club activities and events.

If you have any questions or would like to get involved, feel free to contact us.
